# Potential Improvements

## High Impact (Core Gameplay)

### 1. Enemy Variety
Only one enemy type right now. Adding different enemies per round (e.g., a "Lucky" enemy that gets free rerolls, a "Tanky" goblin with armor, a "Berserker" that deals more damage at low HP) would make each run feel different.

### 2. Round Events / Modifiers
Random modifiers per round like "Double gold round", "Cursed dice (all dice -1)", or "High stakes (instant kill outcomes deal/heal bonus damage)" would add strategic variety.

### 3. Boss Encounters
Every 3-4 rounds, a special boss with a unique mechanic (e.g., copies your last roll, has a shield that blocks one outcome type).

---

## Medium Impact (Progression & Feel)

### 4. Achievement / Milestone System
"Win with only 1 HP", "Roll trips 5 times in a run", etc. Adds replay motivation.

### 5. Difficulty Modes
Easy/Normal/Hard toggle at the start that scales enemy HP, gold rewards, and shop prices.

---

## Low Effort / High Polish

### 6. Keyboard Shortcuts
Press `A` for Attack, `D` for Defend, `R` for Reroll — makes the game feel snappier.
