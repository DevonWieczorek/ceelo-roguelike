# Potential Improvements

## High Impact (Core Gameplay)

### 1. Dice Rolling Animation
Currently dice just show final values. An actual tumbling/spinning animation before revealing results would add tension and excitement to the core mechanic.

### 2. Enemy Variety
Only one enemy type right now. Adding different enemies per round (e.g., a "Lucky" enemy that gets free rerolls, a "Tanky" goblin with armor, a "Berserker" that deals more damage at low HP) would make each run feel different.

### 3. Round Events / Modifiers
Random modifiers per round like "Double gold round", "Cursed dice (all dice -1)", or "High stakes (instant kill outcomes deal/heal bonus damage)" would add strategic variety.

### 4. Boss Encounters
Every 3-4 rounds, a special boss with a unique mechanic (e.g., copies your last roll, has a shield that blocks one outcome type).

---

## Medium Impact (Progression & Feel)

### 5. Floating Damage Numbers
Numbers that pop up and fade when damage is dealt — makes combat feel punchy and responsive.

### 6. Run Stats & History
Track wins/losses, highest round reached, total gold earned, etc. Stored in localStorage. A simple stats screen on the menu.

### 7. Achievement / Milestone System
"Win with only 1 HP", "Roll trips 5 times in a run", etc. Adds replay motivation.

### 8. Difficulty Modes
Easy/Normal/Hard toggle at the start that scales enemy HP, gold rewards, and shop prices.

---

## Low Effort / High Polish

### 9. Screen Transitions
Smooth fade or slide between screens (Menu → PreRound → Combat → Shop) using CSS transitions.

### 10. Sound Improvements
More distinct sounds per enemy type, a dramatic sound for boss encounters, a different melody for a new run high score.

### 11. Keyboard Shortcuts
Press `A` for Attack, `D` for Defend, `R` for Reroll — makes the game feel snappier.
